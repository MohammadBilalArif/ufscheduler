GatorPik The Unofficial UF Scheduler

A tool to help UF students plan their course schedule.

This tool works by scanning your Degree Audit, analyzing the courses you still need to take, and ensuring all prerequisites are met. A tentative list is then generated of which classes you should take in which order.
