University of Florida Scheduler
Welcome to Gator Scheduler.
Here you can input your degree audit information and have your remaining courseload output to you.
◀

Step 1: COMPLETED REQUIREMENTS

1) Click here to go to ISIS.

2) Select Degree Audit on the left.

3) Log in with your gator link.

4) Click on View Critical Tracking Audit.

5) Select all (ctrl+a) then copy (ctrl+c).

6) Paste (ctrl+v) the information into the box to the left.

Step 2: UNMET REQUIREMENTS

1) Click here to go to ISIS.

2) Select Degree Audit on the left.

3) Log in with your gator link.

4) Click on View Unmet Requirements.

5) Select all (ctrl+a) then copy (ctrl+c).

6) Paste (ctrl+v) the information into the box to the right.
